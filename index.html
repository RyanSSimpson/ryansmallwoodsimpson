Summary

Futurist Arthur C. Clark once said: "Any sufficiently advanced technology is indistinguishable from magic". Nothing better embodies this concept than computers, and creatng this magic is what I have dedicated my life to. Afer high school, I formally began this pursuit by undertaking an undergraduate Bachelor of Computer Science at Adelaide University.

Afer graduaton I was eager to take what I'd learnt into the real world. Afer declining an invitaton to do honours, I immediately founded Krion Games with a few other alumni with whom I shared a similar work ethic and passion for video game development. Six months later, we released our frst game, Greg's Eggventure, for iPhone and Android mobile devices. Following this success, we began development on our second game, Scale Seeker, which we entered in Adelaide University's 2016 Tech eChallenge, where it won the competton and us a trip to Microsoft HQ in Seattle to speak about it.

I am a results-focused individual, and have worked to successful results on both solo projects and as part of larger teams. Founding a company has taught me to handle a large variety of tasks- both sofware and business related- and to give each its due diligence. I have competed in athletcs all of my life, and this has taught me the discipline required to persevere with harder, long term goals which require a consistent work ethic to complete.
